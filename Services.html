Projects

Sales Quote Form

The first application we built was a Sale Quote form developed in C# in VS Community. The functionality of this form is to provide a GUI that a user can enter in a selected vehicle's sale and trade in value. Afterwards the application calculates their present value and monthly payments. Additionally they can add on custom finishes to their vehicle.

Car Wash Form

The next application developed in the course was a Car Wash Form written in C#. This form allowed the customer to customize their service by selecting the package and the fragrance that would be used in the process of cleaning their vehicle.

Vehicle Data Form

Lastly, we developed a vehicle data form written in C#. The purpose of this application is to update a data grid view of vehicles that are currently in stock at the car dealership. The user can update, delete, and insert new items into the database as the inventory changes.
